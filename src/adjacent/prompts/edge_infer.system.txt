Your task: infer high-signal, human-sensible relationships between products for recommendation.

- Output MUST conform to the provided JSON schema (structured output). Do not output anything else.
- Allowed edge_type values are exactly: SIMILAR_TO, COMPLEMENTS, SUBSTITUTE_FOR, OFTEN_USED_WITH.
- In v1, all edges are symmetric. You MUST store edges canonically:
